# Custom Raceday

Race Coordinator AI (RC AI) allows you to completely customize the raceday experience, including overriding the default raceday pages to suit your needs.

## Overriding Raceday Pages

RC AI provides several built-in pages that govern the raceday workflow, such as the main raceday page, leaderboard, and heat results. You can override these pages to display your own custom UI.

### Using the UI Editor

The [UI Editor](ui-editor.md) is the primary tool for creating and modifying custom raceday pages. 

1. Open the UI Editor.
2. Select the page you want to customize (e.g., Raceday Page).
3. Modify the layout, elements, and data bindings to create your custom design.
4. Save your changes.

### Custom Themes

You can also apply [Themes](themes.md) to further customize the look and feel of your custom raceday pages, incorporating your own graphics, colors, and branding.

## Configuring Custom Pages

Once you have created your custom pages, you can configure RC AI to use them during a race event. This involves mapping your custom UI to the appropriate race states and events within the race configuration.
